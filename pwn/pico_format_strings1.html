In this challange we were provided with 2 files, one containing program code and other one was an ELF executable.
After a quick look at a source code, I noticed the goal of the challange:

Now I had to print the contents of the secrets and flag.
I looked it up on the Internet and as it turns out some %lx (with a separator for easier distinction) formatted strings printed out the values in hex.

Then I parsed the hex into ASCII, and had to reverse the order of retreived values and received a flag!
